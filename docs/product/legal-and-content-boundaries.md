# Legal and content boundaries

This is a product policy, not formal legal advice. Obtain qualified legal review for launch markets, source licensing, subscriptions, privacy, and claims.

## Positioning

The product is an independent preparation service and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Swedish public authorities. UI, store listings, support, and advertising must not imply official status. Readiness scores summarize observed practice performance; they are uncertain, are not official assessments, and do not guarantee passing or any immigration outcome.

The product provides educational material, not immigration legal advice. Questions about individual eligibility, rights, deadlines, or legal strategy must direct users to authoritative sources or qualified advisers.

## Content boundaries

- Do not solicit, store, or publish leaked examination questions.
- Do not copy official question banks or third-party study materials without permission.
- Distinguish unprotected facts from protected selection, arrangement, wording, images, and explanations; write original assessment material.
- Maintain internal source traceability for every published question, including version and access metadata.
- Respect licenses, attribution requirements, removal requests, and source update dates.
- Present unresolved examination format, scoring, timing, and coverage as unknown until verified.

See the editorial controls in [content strategy](content-strategy.md) and immutable versioning in [ADR-003](../decisions/ADR-003-content-versioning.md).

## Privacy and minimization

Collect only data needed for accounts, learning history, service safety, subscription fulfillment, and explicitly enabled analytics. Avoid collecting citizenship case details, immigration documents, national identity numbers, precise location, contacts, unrelated demographics, or free-form sensitive data. Separate operational telemetry from learning content, limit retention, support GDPR export/deletion, and do not send personal or special-category data to AI providers unless a reviewed feature has a lawful, documented need and suitable controls. See [security and privacy](../architecture/security-and-privacy.md).
