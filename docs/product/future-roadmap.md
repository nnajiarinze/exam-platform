# Future roadmap

## Current product

The MVP is citizenship-only: mobile learning and mock flows, admin content operations, versioned publishing, learner progress, subscriptions, and constrained AI assistance. The planned public web application is deferred.

## Later opportunities

- Language-learning support tied to verified content, after validating whether it improves citizenship preparation without confusing assessment mastery with language ability.
- B2B organization accounts, cohort assignment, aggregate reporting, and invoicing, subject to privacy and demand validation.
- Swedish driving-theory preparation as a separate exam product after the platform proves reusable.

Reusable capabilities should include exam/version configuration, topic taxonomy, fact and source traceability, question versioning, release import, sessions, deterministic scoring, mastery evidence, localization, issue workflows, entitlements, and AI job controls.

## Do not build driving-specific capabilities now

Do not implement road-sign media models, vehicle classes, driving hazard simulations, official driving blueprint assumptions, driving-specific scoring, or driving authority integrations. Do not generalize interfaces speculatively when citizenship requirements provide no concrete second use case.

## Gate for another exam product

Add another product only when citizenship content and learner flows are operationally stable; platform seams have measured reuse value; product demand and acquisition economics are validated; authoritative sources and licensing are available; exam rules are confirmed; editorial expertise and review capacity exist; legal/privacy impact is reviewed; and the new product can use isolated configuration/content without weakening historical reproducibility. Record the decision in a new ADR and product brief.
